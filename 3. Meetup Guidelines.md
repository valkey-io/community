# Run a Local Valkey Meetup

Valkey Meetup Groups are community-driven meetups and recurring gatherings dedicated to growing awareness, sharing knowledge, and strengthening the open source ecosystem around Valkey. These groups bring together developers, operators, contributors, and advocates to discuss real-world use cases, share best practices, and build lasting local community presence.


---

## Planning a Meetup

If you're planning a meetup, start here: **Read the [Full Meetup Charter](https://valkey.io/community/meetup-groups/)**

## Promotion and Support

To get official support:
- Fill out the [meetup social promo request form]( https://form.asana.com/?k=9zh9lhK3yWLavaOi6C0hgA&d=9283783873717 ) 
- Share your event details early for amplification

**Once submitted, the Valkey community team can help with:**
- Social media promotion
- Event visibility
- Community coordination

---

## Tips for Successful Meetups

- Keep talks practical and experience-based  
- Include live demonstrations when possible  
- Provide time for networking  
- Encourage participation from newcomers  

---

## Community Impact

Local meetups are a key part of the Valkey ecosystem. They help:
- Strengthen regional developer communities  
- Surface real-world feedback for the project  
- Build connections between users and maintainers
