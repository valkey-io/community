# Community Videos for the Valkey YouTube Channel

This is the process for submitting community videos to the official Valkey YouTube channel. It covers short form demos, long form demos, and other video ideas from the community.

Overview
* Discuss your idea in this [Slack channel](https://valkey-oss-developer.slack.com/archives/C0BEU4MV40Y)
* Submit your video through the Asana form
* LF does a first pass review
* A volunteer video reviewer does the final pass
* Approved videos get published to the Valkey YouTube channel

## Step 1: Discuss Your Idea

Before submitting, bring your idea to the [#valkey-video-workshop](https://valkey-oss-developer.slack.com/archives/C0BEU4MV40Y) on Valkey Slack.

Use this space to:

* Pitch short form demo ideas (quick feature walkthroughs, tips, one-off tricks)
* Pitch long form demo ideas (deep dives, tutorials, architecture walkthroughs)
* Get feedback on scope, format, and framing before you record
* Find collaborators if you want to co-create a video
* Ask questions about what the community and maintainers want to see

**This step is optional but strongly recommended. Getting early feedback saves you from reshoots and increases the odds your video gets accepted.**

## Step 2: Submit Your Video

Once your video is ready, submit it through the [Valkey Video Submission](https://form.asana.com/?k=9zh9lhK3yWLavaOi6C0hgA&d=9283783873717) Form.

We use an Asana form instead of Google Drive because Google Drive is restricted in some regions. The Asana form lets contributors submit from anywhere, including restricted regions.

Include in your submission:

* Video file or link
* Title and short description
* Format (short form or long form)
* Any relevant context (topic, tools used, target audience)
* Your name and preferred contact method

## Step 3: LF First Pass Review

The Linux Foundation team does the first pass review. This checks for:

* Messaging accuracy
* Appropriate content and vendor neutral
* Basic production quality
* Alignment with Valkey branding guidelines

Videos that pass this stage move on to final review. Videos that need work get feedback sent back to the contributor.

## Step 4: Volunteer Reviewer Final Pass

After the LF first pass, a volunteer video reviewer from the community does the final review. This is a second set of eyes to confirm:

* Content is accurate and up to date
* Video is ready for public publishing
* Nothing was missed in the first pass

Want to be a volunteer video reviewer? Say so in the [#valkey-video-workshop](https://valkey-oss-developer.slack.com/archives/C0BEU4MV40Y) Slack channel.

Step 5: Publishing

Once a video clears both review passes, it gets scheduled for publishing on the official Valkey YouTube channel.
